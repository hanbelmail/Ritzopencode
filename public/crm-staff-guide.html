Status Workflow

Every reservation should move through these statuses in order unless it is cancelled.

1
QUOTE REQUESTED

Guest requested a quote

The ticket exists, but staff has not sent the private rate yet. The guest can view the ticket but cannot submit payment.

  • Staff reviews guest names, dates, room type, email, phone, and notes.
  • New quote staff alert should notify active staff recipients.
  • Next staff action is adding pricing and moving to PRICE SENT.
2
PRICE SENT

Private rate is ready

Staff added the retail price and private rate. The guest can now see pricing and submit payment proof.

  • Attach the Ritz website retail price screenshot when available.
  • The price-sent guest email sends the ticket link and quote details once.
  • If staff copy is enabled, active staff recipients also receive a copy.
3
PAYMENT SUBMITTED

Guest uploaded payment proof

The guest selected a payment method and uploaded a screenshot. The ticket is waiting for staff verification.

  • Staff should open the payment proof and confirm payment details.
  • Payment-submitted alert should notify active staff recipients.
  • After approval, staff moves the ticket to PAYMENT VERIFIED.
4
PAYMENT VERIFIED

Payment is approved

Staff verified the payment. The guest sees that payment cleared and final booking confirmation is next.

  • Dates become blocked in the reservation date picker.
  • Analytics counts the ticket as booked revenue.
  • Next staff action is finalizing the booking with the hotel.
5
BOOKING CONFIRMED

Reservation is finalized

The booking is complete. The guest sees a finalized reservation message.

  • Booking-confirmed hotel alert should send to active hotel recipients.
  • Dates remain blocked in the reservation date picker.
  • Analytics counts the ticket as booked revenue.
!
CANCELLED

Reservation is no longer active

The ticket remains visible for history, but the guest cannot pay and should request a new quote if needed.

  • Use Cancel when the reservation should remain in CRM history.
  • Use Delete only for duplicate, test, or incorrect tickets that should be removed.
  • Cancelled dates are not blocked as confirmed reservations.

Daily Staff Process

Morning Review

  • Open the Dashboard and filter by QUOTE REQUESTED.
  • Check guest details, dates, room type, and notes.
  • Prioritize urgent check-in dates first.

Quote Preparation

  • Confirm retail price and discount.
  • Attach the Ritz retail price screenshot.
  • Save as PRICE SENT and confirm email delivery toast.

Payment Review

  • Filter by PAYMENT SUBMITTED.
  • Open payment proof screenshot and verify funds.
  • Mark PAYMENT VERIFIED only after payment is confirmed.

Booking Closeout

  • Finalize the reservation with the hotel.
  • Mark BOOKING CONFIRMED after hotel confirmation.
  • Use Analytics to review booked revenue and pending value.

Common Mistakes To Avoid

Sending Price Without Retail Price

PRICE SENT requires a valid retail price. If pricing is not ready, keep the ticket in QUOTE REQUESTED.

Skipping Retail Screenshot

The CRM allows sending without a screenshot after confirmation, but staff should attach the screenshot whenever possible for trust and clarity.

Confirming Too Early

Do not mark BOOKING CONFIRMED before payment is verified and the hotel booking is finalized.

Deleting Instead Of Cancelling

Use CANCELLED for real guest reservations that fell through. Delete only duplicate, test, or wrong tickets.

Suggested Additions For Training

These items can be added to future staff SOPs or CRM screens to make the workflow even clearer.

Payment Proof Review Checklist

Add a checklist for amount, sender name, date, method, and matching ticket ID before marking PAYMENT VERIFIED.

Retail Screenshot Checklist

Require staff to confirm the screenshot shows the same room, dates, retail total, and Ritz source.

Email Failure SOP

Explain what staff should do when an email alert fails, such as manually contacting the guest or checking Email Dashboard settings.

Lead Priority Rules

Define how staff should prioritize near check-in dates, high-value bookings, repeat guests, and payment-submitted tickets.

Cancel vs Delete Policy

Document that cancelled reservations stay in reports and guest history, while deleted tickets disappear from the CRM.

Guest Follow-Up Templates

Add message templates for quote reminders, payment reminders, missing screenshot requests, and booking confirmation follow-up.
